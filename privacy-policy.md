# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Chrome extension "Tien Minh Auto Flow".

## Information Collection and Use
The extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.
All data generated by the user (such as prompts, settings, and automation states) is processed locally within the user's browser.

## Data Storage
The extension may use Chrome local storage to save user preferences, license status, and usage configuration.
No data is sent to external servers.

## Permissions
Requested permissions are used strictly to provide the core functionality of the extension, including automation on Google Flow, user interface rendering, and local data storage.
No permissions are used to collect personal data.

## Third-Party Services
The extension does not integrate with or share data with third-party services.

## Data Security
All operations are performed locally on the user's device. No remote data transmission occurs.

## Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
